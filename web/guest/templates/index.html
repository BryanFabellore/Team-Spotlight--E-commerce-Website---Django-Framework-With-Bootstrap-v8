About Fleeky Curtains

Fleeky curtains is currently an online business shop that provides comfort, joy, and warm satisfaction to the customers with regards to their curtain needs. It was established in 2021 and currently operates and manufactures its products locally. Our expertise and experience go hand-in-hand, strengthening our business in catering services and products directly to our clients' needs. We strive to provide top-notch services, products, and purchase experiences to consumers of all income levels. Fleeky curtains also aims to grow its market share in the home product industry by observing and adapting to consumer behavior. Our business values and qualities are reflected in the observable morals and qualities of our leading members. We are committed to delivering high-quality products to the general public.
